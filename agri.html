Maintainance

How to Work on Agrivoltaics:

  • Install solar panels 6–8 ft above ground for dappled shading.
  • Alternate open-sun and shaded beds for comparison (pilot: 2-bed setup).
  • Sensors (soil moisture, pH, temperature, light) connected to Raspberry Pi / ESP32.
  • Data feeds into app for irrigation scheduling & weekly PDF reports.
