Crop Rotation

To maintain soil nutrients & reduce pests, rotate the following beds annually:

Bed A — Leafy Greens → Legumes → Root Crops
  • Year 1: Kale, lettuce
  • Year 2: Beans / peas (nitrogen fixers)
  • Year 3: Carrots, beets

Bed B — Fruiting Crops → Herbs → Cover Crop
  • Year 1: Tomatoes, peppers
  • Year 2: Basil, cilantro
  • Year 3: Cover crop (clover) for nitrogen enrichment
